Lightview

Our product, called “Lightview” is a device that reveals bacteria and other transmissible microorganisms, when putting your hands into it. The idea is that the device will turn on with the help of a sensor, connected with UV-light that detects microorganisms. A camera that projects your hands to a screen at the top will allow users to visually see where the microorganisms are and if it is a bacteria or a virus, and hopefully with cutting edge technology what kind of virus or bacteria there are.
