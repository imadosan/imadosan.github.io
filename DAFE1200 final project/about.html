About us

Anisave is the animals' advocate.

Anisave is an international company that focuses on stopping animal extinction. Through the help of our advanced technology, you will be able to track the animal once you adopted it. Using our interactive app, you get access to all necessary information about the animal. Our goal is to prevent the degradation of natural areas where animals live and create the best living conditions for these animals.

The Anisave app helps to increase the feeling of closeness to wild animals and nature in a completely new way, which can help to raise awareness about endangered species.
