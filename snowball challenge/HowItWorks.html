How it works

Our solution to fix this problem is quite simple. We want to use drones to locate this plastic. Drones can fly over the ocean surface and through a camera, we can see the plastic, and via a GPS we will know where the plastic is, and we can procede to go and and pick up the trash with big boats. In the same manner, electrical underwater 'submarine' drones can locate plastic that has sinken below the ocean surface. Since it is harder to collect sinken plastic via boat, the 'submarine' drones could be built to collect the plastic it selves. This way it will have to empty it selves after a while and the process would be longer, but in return the process would also be easier.
