Our goal

Our long term goal is to make stop global warming. Especially we want to stop the constaint polution of the ocean. We love all kinds of animals, from your own dog or cat, to the wanderers of the ocean floor and the birds high up in the sky. with todays habits, way to much plastic and other harmfull waste end up in the ocean, and a lot of marine life suffer as a consequence. According to Ocean crusaders and National Geographic it is believed that there are 5.25 trillion pieces of plastic debris in the ocean. Of that mass, 269,000 tons float on the surface, while some four billion plastic microfibers per square kilometer litter the deep sea.
